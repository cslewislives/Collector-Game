Horcrux Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four Horcruxes below. By clicking on a Horcrux you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you Lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

The value of each Horcrux is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts the value of each Horcrux will change.
